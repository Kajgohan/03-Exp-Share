Instructions

The purpose of this activity is to study popout! We want to see how good you are at spotting an outlier! But really, since we are going to extrapolate this data and assume that you lot are the least unique/extraordinary lot possible, we are going to see how good these outliers are at being spotted ________ by you! Your job is simple: As soon as you hit the Start button, a scatter plot will appear over most of the screen for a split second, so be ready. Your goal is to determine if there were any outliers. Then, on the following page, all you have to do is indicate if there was an outlier or not. Press Q for yes and P for no, and the test will automatically run again. Then, when you are bored you can click to exit. Thanks for participating!
